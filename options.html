Libro.fm ALC Helper Settings

What this extension does:

  • Identifies Owned Books: When you view a monthly ALC list, it loads your library and grays out any books you have already purchased. Note: Your library may take some time to load initially, but once complete, it is saved locally for 24 hours and applied instantly to any other ALC pages you visit.
  • Adds Genre Tags: It looks at the books on the current ALC page and appends their specific genre tags directly to the listing for easy reference.

Loading Status Popup

When you visit an ALC page, a small status box will appear in the top right corner of your screen to keep you updated:

  • Library Sync: Displays whether it is currently loading your library (and how many books it has found so far) or shows the total number of books already loaded. It also includes a "Refresh Library" button so you can manually force an update if you recently acquired a new book.
  • Genre Tagging: Indicates whether the extension is actively looking up genre information for the current page, or if it has finished attaching all the genre tags.
